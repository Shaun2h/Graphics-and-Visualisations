arguments: <'t','e', or 'r' depending on trapzoidal, forward euler of rk4 stepper> <'s', 'p' or 'c', depending if you want simple,pendulum or cloth> <step size. any float value>
example argument:   t s 0.01
Spawns a trapzoidal simple system with 0.01 step size.
press R to reset at any time.
No extra components (unless RK4 counts)
